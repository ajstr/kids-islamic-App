Effective date: [05 12, 2026]

## Children’s Privacy
The App is designed for children and families. Because we do not collect any data, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under any age. If you believe we have inadvertently received personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

## Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or social plugins that collect user data. If the App includes links to external websites or content in the future, those sites operate under their own privacy policies.

Because we do not collect or store any personal data, there are no user records to secure. The App runs locally on your device.
